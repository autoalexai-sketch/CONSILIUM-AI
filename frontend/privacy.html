Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026  ·  Effective: July 1, 2026
Short version: We collect only what is necessary to run the service. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for advertising. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

1. Who we are

CONSILIUM AI is operated by Oleksandr Latyntsev, an individual entrepreneur based in Kraków, Poland (EU). For privacy matters, contact us at: privacy@ai-consilium.com

As we are based in Poland (European Union), this policy is designed to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR, Regulation (EU) 2016/679).

2. Data we collect and why

Account data

  • Email address — required to create an account and send you important service notifications (e.g. welcome email, account changes). Legal basis: contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Password — stored as a bcrypt hash. We never store your plain-text password and cannot recover it.
  • Credit balance — integer counter tracking your remaining deliberation credits.

Content you create

  • Queries / messages — text you send to the Board of Directors during deliberation sessions.
  • Decision Journal entries — titles, query text, and AI-generated verdicts you choose to save.
  • Personal Principles — notes you add to guide the Council's reasoning.
  • Knowledge Vault (Wiki) pages — notes and summaries you create.

This content is stored to provide the core service. It is used to personalise responses via the Context Gateway (injecting your Principles and past decisions into prompts). Legal basis: contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).

Usage data

  • Session logs — timestamps, latency, credit usage, coherence scores. Used to improve service quality and detect abuse.
  • IP addresses — temporarily stored in memory for rate limiting (max 5 minutes). Not persisted to database.

Payment data

  • Credit purchases are processed by Stripe. We store only the Stripe session ID, package ID, credit amount, and payment status. Full card details are never sent to our servers.
  • Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

3. AI providers — data sent to third parties

To generate deliberation responses, your query text is sent to one or more of the following AI providers. Each provider receives only the minimum context needed to generate a response:

  • Groq — Privacy policy
  • Anthropic (Claude) — Privacy policy
  • DeepSeek — Privacy policy
  • OpenRouter — Privacy policy
  • Google (Gemini) — Privacy policy

We recommend not including sensitive personal information (names, ID numbers, medical data, financial account details) in your queries.

4. Data storage and retention

  • Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS RDS (eu-north-1, Stockholm region, EU).
  • The application runs on Render.com (US-based infrastructure). Data in transit is encrypted via TLS.
  • We retain your account data and content for as long as your account is active.
  • Session logs are retained for 90 days, then deleted automatically.
  • After account deletion, all your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

As an EU resident, you have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — delete your account and all associated data. You can do this from the app settings, or by emailing us.
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON).
  • Restriction — request that we stop processing your data while a dispute is resolved.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@ai-consilium.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Polish Data Protection Authority (UODO): uodo.gov.pl

6. Cookies

We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. The application stores your authentication token and UI preferences (theme) in localStorage on your device. This data never leaves your browser except as part of API requests to our own server.

7. Children

CONSILIUM AI is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe we have done so, contact us and we will delete the data immediately.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date and notify registered users by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of the service after that date constitutes acceptance.
