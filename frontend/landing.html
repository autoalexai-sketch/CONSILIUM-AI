Pipeline

6 phases.
Raw data to verified decision.

01
Discovery — Cognitive Classification
Your query is classified across 5 dimensions: complexity, urgency, emotional load, domain, language. The right directors are selected automatically.
CognitiveClassifier
02
Evidence — Scout scans sources
Intelligence gathering from verified sources. Facts labeled with confidence markers. Gaps explicitly noted.
Scout · Perplexity
03
Analysis — Patterns and structure
Analyst decomposes the query, maps cause-effect chains, identifies tension points.
Analyst · DeepSeek
04
Architecture — Solution design
Multiple approaches compared (A/B/C). MVP-first roadmap. Devil's Advocate stress-tests everything.
Architect · Devil's Advocate
05
Quality Gate — Coherence check
Synthesizer audits director outputs for contradictions. Low coherence triggers VETO and forces revision.
Synthesizer · Verifier
06
Verdict — Chairman's final decision
Clear recommendation, concrete next steps, success criteria. Saved to your Decision Journal.
Chairman · Decision Journal
